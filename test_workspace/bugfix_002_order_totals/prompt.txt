Some customers are getting too much discount at checkout.

When a percentage coupon is applied, the final total is lower than expected for certain orders. Support suspects the same coupon effect may be getting applied twice somewhere in the checkout flow.

Expected behavior:
- a percentage coupon should affect the order only once;
- promotional items must never receive the coupon;
- invalid coupon codes should be ignored;
- the final total should still be rounded to two decimals.

Please investigate the checkout flow and fix the bug without changing the intended pricing rules.

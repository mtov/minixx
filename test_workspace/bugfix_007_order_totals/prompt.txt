We have a bug in checkout totals.

Customer support reported that some orders are getting the wrong final amount when coupons and shipping rules are combined.
The most common complaints are:

- promotional items are being treated incorrectly when percentage coupons are used;
- free shipping is sometimes lost after a coupon is applied, even when the cart originally qualified for it;
- some totals look like they are being rounded at the wrong step.

Please investigate `calculate_order_total(items, coupon_code)` in `src/checkout.py` and fix the behavior without changing the intended pricing rules.

Do not modify the tests.
Run: `python -m pytest -q`.

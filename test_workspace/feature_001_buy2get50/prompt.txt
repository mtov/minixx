The checkout team wants to support a new promotion code: `BUY2GET50`.

Please implement that feature in the checkout flow.

Expected behavior:
- only items marked as eligible may participate in the promotion;
- clearance items must never count toward the promotion and must never receive the discount;
- quantities count as repeated units;
- for each group of three eligible units, apply a 50% discount to the cheapest eligible unit in that group;
- invalid promotion codes should be ignored;
- the final total should still be rounded to two decimals.

Do not change the intended non-promotional behavior.
